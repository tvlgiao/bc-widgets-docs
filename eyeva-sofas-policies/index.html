    Our Policies Block Widget¶

    Display a neat row of store policies — like safe delivery, easy returns, or warranty — each shown with an icon and a short line of text. It's a quick way to build shopper trust on your home page or product pages.

    Page Builder name: Our Policies Block | Eyeva Sofas — Version: 1.0.1

    Add it to your page¶

    1. In your store admin, open Storefront → My Themes and click Customize to launch Page Builder.
    2. In the Widgets panel on the left, find Our Policies Block | Eyeva Sofas.
    3. Drag it onto your page. It appears with two sample policy items.
    4. With the widget selected, open the settings panel on the right to customize it.

    Settings¶

    Settings¶

    General¶

    Option What it does Default
    Heading text The title shown above the policy row. You type the text and format it with the visual editor. Our policies are top-notch
    Style Inherit theme (match your theme's fonts, colors, and spacing) or Custom (set your own below). Inherit theme
    Base spacing Overall spacing scale for the block (in rem). Custom style only. 1 rem
    Heading font size Heading size on desktop, in pixels. Custom style only. 28
    Heading font size on mobile Heading size on phones, in pixels. Custom style only. 24
    Item background color Background color of each policy card. Custom style only. #ffffff
    Item border color Border color of each policy card. Custom style only. #dddddd
    Item border radius How rounded the card corners are, in pixels. Custom style only. 4
    Icon color Color of the policy icons. Custom style only. #76774A
    Text color Color of the policy text. Custom style only. #555555
    Font size Policy text size on desktop, in pixels. Custom style only. 18
    Font size on mobile Policy text size on phones, in pixels. Custom style only. 16

    Policy items¶

    The Items tab holds the list of policies. Each item shows an icon and a short line of text.

    • Add an item: click the add button at the bottom of the Items list.
    • Remove an item: open the item and use its delete (trash) control.
    • Reorder items: drag an item up or down in the list to change the display order.

    The widget starts with two items. Each item has these options:

    Option What it does Default
    SVG Icon HTML The icon shown for this policy. Paste icon artwork here, or keep the built-in icon. A built-in icon
    HTML text The policy text. You type it and format it with the visual editor. Safe & convenient delivery

    Tips¶

    • Keep each policy text short — two or three words or one short line reads best in the row.
    • Three to six items usually looks best; too many can crowd the row on smaller screens.
    • Use a consistent icon style across all items for a clean, professional look.
    • Put your most important policy first, since it reads first from left to right.
    • Leave Style on Inherit theme so the block matches your store; switch to Custom only when you need specific colors or sizes.
