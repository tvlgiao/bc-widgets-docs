    Section Templates Gallery¶

    The AI HTML Widget comes with a growing library of ready-made section templates you can add to your BigCommerce store with a single click. Pick a template, drop it onto your page, then use the AI assistant to tweak the colors, text, images, and layout until it matches your brand.

    This page showcases the Form Templates and Popup Templates collections. Many more templates — hero banners, product showcases, testimonials, and more — are available inside the Browse Blocks gallery in the AI assistant.

    How to Use a Template¶

    1. Add the AI HTML Widget to your page in Page Builder.
    2. Open the AI assistant by clicking the purple AI button.
    3. Click Browse Blocks below the message box.
    4. Filter by category — for example Form Templates or Popup Templates — or search by name.
    5. Click a template to preview it with Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile views.
    6. Click Use This Block to add it to your page.
    7. Tell the assistant what to change, such as "Change the colors to match my brand."

    For a full walkthrough, see the AI HTML Widget guide.

Each one is responsive and includes built-in timing — appearing after a delay, when a visitor is about to leave, or on a click — and remembers when a visitor has dismissed it so it does not keep reappearing.

    Customizing a Template¶

    After you apply a template, use the AI assistant to make it your own. Just describe what you want in plain English. For example:

    • "Change the background color to #1a1a2e."
    • "Replace the placeholder text with our actual shipping policy."
    • "Update the discount code to SUMMER25."
    • "Show the popup after 10 seconds instead of 5."
    • "Add our logo to the popup header."

    Tip

    A template is just a starting point. The assistant can change anything — colors, fonts, layout, text, images, and timing. Describe what you want and it updates the template for you.

    Popup Timing¶

    Popup templates appear in one of three ways, and you can ask the assistant to switch between them after applying a template.

    Timing How it works Best for
    After a delay The popup appears a few seconds after the visitor arrives. Welcome offers, announcements
    On exit The popup appears when the visitor moves to leave the page. Last-chance offers, cart reminders
    On click The popup appears when a specific button or link is clicked. Size guides, store locators

    Every popup also remembers when a visitor has closed it, so it won't keep popping up during their visit.
